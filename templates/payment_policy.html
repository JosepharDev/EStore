Payment Policy

Effective Date: January 25, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to EStore. This Payment Policy explains the terms and conditions related to the payment for images, services, and products on our website. By making a purchase, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

2. Accepted Payment Methods

We accept a variety of payment methods to ensure a smooth purchasing experience. These include:

  • Credit/Debit Cards: We accept major credit and debit cards, including Visa, MasterCard, American Express, and Discover.
  • PayPal: We offer secure payments through PayPal for easy checkout.
  • Bank Transfers: For larger purchases, we may offer bank transfer options (please contact us for more details).
  • Other Methods: Additional payment methods may be added from time to time to accommodate our customers.

3. Payment Security

Your security is our priority. All payment transactions on EStore are processed through secure, encrypted payment gateways. We do not store your sensitive financial information. Payments are securely handled by trusted third-party processors, such as PayPal or Stripe, ensuring your data is protected.

4. Currency and Pricing

All prices listed on EStore are in the specified currency (e.g., USD, EUR) and are subject to change without notice. The final price will be displayed at checkout, and any applicable taxes or fees will be added at that time.

5. Taxes

Depending on your location, you may be subject to local taxes, including sales tax or VAT. These taxes will be calculated and added to your order during checkout if applicable. EStore is not responsible for any additional taxes or fees imposed by your local jurisdiction.

6. Payment Confirmation

After completing a payment, you will receive an email confirmation containing the details of your transaction, including the purchase amount, payment method, and order information. If you do not receive a confirmation, please contact our customer support team.

7. Order Processing

Once your payment is confirmed, we will begin processing your order. Please allow up to 24 hours for processing and delivery of digital products. If there is an issue with your payment, we may contact you for clarification or further action.

8. Refunds and Cancellations

Due to the nature of digital products, all sales are final. We do not offer refunds or cancellations once a transaction has been processed. However, if you encounter any issues with your order, please contact our support team, and we will do our best to resolve the issue in a timely manner.

9. Payment Disputes

If you have an issue with a payment or wish to dispute a charge, please contact us within 30 days of the transaction. We will investigate the issue and, if necessary, work with our payment processors to resolve the dispute.

EStore reserves the right to modify this Payment Policy at any time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Effective Date." It is your responsibility to review this page periodically for any updates or changes.
