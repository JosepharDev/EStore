Shipping Policy

Effective Date: January 25, 2025

1. Introduction

At EStore, we are committed to delivering your purchased products in a timely and efficient manner. This Shipping Policy outlines our practices regarding how we ship products, delivery timeframes, and any associated fees. Please read this policy carefully before making a purchase.

2. Digital Products

For digital products (such as images), there is no physical shipping involved. After your purchase is confirmed, you will receive an email with a download link for the digital files. You can download your purchased files directly from our website as well.

3. Physical Products (if applicable)

If we offer physical products (e.g., prints), the following applies:

  • Processing Time: Orders for physical products are processed within 1-2 business days after payment confirmation.
  • Shipping Time: Delivery times vary depending on your location. Standard shipping typically takes 5-7 business days for domestic orders. International orders may take longer, depending on customs and shipping carriers.
  • Shipping Fees: Shipping fees will be calculated during checkout based on the weight, size, and destination of the order.

4. Shipping Methods

We offer various shipping methods to suit your needs. Available methods will depend on your location and the type of product you're purchasing. Common options include:

  • Standard Shipping: Available for most orders and typically arrives within 5-7 business days (domestic orders).
  • Expedited Shipping: For faster delivery, we offer expedited shipping options. Delivery times are typically 2-3 business days (domestic orders).
  • International Shipping: We ship to a variety of international locations. Delivery times and fees will vary depending on the destination country.

5. Order Tracking

Once your order has been shipped, you will receive a shipping confirmation email with tracking information (if applicable). You can use the tracking number to track your shipment through the relevant carrier's website.

6. Shipping Restrictions

We are currently unable to ship to P.O. Boxes, military addresses (APO, FPO, DPO), or certain international locations. If your address is restricted, you will be notified during the checkout process.

7. Shipping Delays

While we strive to deliver your orders promptly, certain factors such as weather conditions, holidays, or carrier delays may cause shipping delays. We are not responsible for delays caused by the shipping carrier or circumstances beyond our control. We will do our best to notify you in case of any significant delays.

8. Lost or Damaged Items

If your order is lost or damaged during transit, please contact us immediately. We will assist you in filing a claim with the carrier or taking appropriate action to resolve the issue. Please retain all packaging and shipping materials as they may be needed for the claim process.

9. Address Errors

It is your responsibility to provide the correct shipping address. If a package is returned due to an incorrect address, you will be responsible for any additional shipping fees incurred. Please ensure your shipping information is accurate before submitting your order.

10. Changes to the Shipping Policy

EStore reserves the right to modify or update this Shipping Policy at any time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Effective Date." We encourage you to review this policy periodically for any updates or changes.
